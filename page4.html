Monster Mash


For our group project, we decided to do a Monster Mash. Each person chose five monsters that are known in their culture. Since I come from a Mexican background, I decided to choose the very well known Chupacabras, La llorona, El duende,El Cucuy, and Siguanaba.


My pieces are in green wood with a few in clear acrylic
