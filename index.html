Assignment 2

Two artworks made in Class and one made outside of class. The last image is based on an assignment I did for another class in which our topic was about Microscopic and Macroscopic. I drew two hands in fists chained up by DNA strands. I making this assignment I thought about how we are affected in the microscopic level by our own individuality and in a Macro sense how it affects us as a whole. We are simply chained to our bodies- that of not being able to control what happens In the end. We are all prone to getting sick- its us vs our biology. the fact that i know someone who has experienced Cancer, inspired me in creating this art.
